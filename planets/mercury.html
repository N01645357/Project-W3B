Mercury

Mercury is the first planet from the Sun. It's the closest to it and the smallest planet in the solar system, being around 1/3 the width of Earth. It's the fastest planet, completing a full rotation around the Sun in 88 Earth days. This bears a parallel to it's namesake, the Roman god, Mercury, who's known for their swiftness. It's the second densest planet and it's surface is solid, rocky, and cratered. Unlike most planets, it doesn't have an atmosphere. Because of this, Mercury experiences extreme temperature variations.
